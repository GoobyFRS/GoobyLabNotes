# Client Site Visit Protocols

## Scheduling and Preparation

- Schedule visits during client's regular business hours unless otherwise requested.
- Confirm appointment 24 hours in advance.
- Review client history and current issues before arrival.
- Bring necessary tools and documentation.
- Dress according to client's business environment.

### On-Site Conduct

- Arrive 5-10 minutes early.
- Check in with client's designated contact.
- Wear company identification.
- Follow client's security and safety protocols.
- Document all work performed.
- Clean work area before departing.

### Safety and Security

- Never share client access credentials.
- Follow proper data handling procedures.
- Report any security concerns immediately.
- Obtain necessary permissions before accessing restricted areas.
- Document any incidents or accidents.

### Post-Visit Procedures

- Complete visit documentation before leaving site.
- Review completed work with client representative.
- Provide summary of work performed and next steps.
- Update client documentation in company systems.
- Follow up within 24 hours to ensure satisfaction.

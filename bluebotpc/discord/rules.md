# Welcome to our Sever

_The Discord Server of BlueBotPC, a technology company based in North Dakota._

As a new user here, we want you to have a fantastic experience. Take a moment and get familiar with our guidelines:

**Good Vibes only:**
Keep the vibes positive! There's no room for harassment or hate speech here. Let's make everyone feel welcome. Do not DM users without their permission first.

**Avoid the Spam-Jam:**
Keep the chat neat - dodge the flood of emojis, novels, or tagging without a cause. Let's keep conversations easy to follow!

**No NSFW content:**
Keep our server family-friendly. Any inappropriate material will be swiftly removed.

**Shh, it's a secret:**
Remember, our server is public. Let's keep sensitive info off the chat to protect our privacy and technology stacks. BlueBotPC will always share passwords via Bitwarden Send.

Friendly reminder that we do follow the [Discord Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and abide [Discord Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).

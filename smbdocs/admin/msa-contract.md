# MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

## Managed IT Services

This Master Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between BlueBotPC LLC ("Provider") and the client identified in the accompanying Service Order ("Client"), effective as of the date of execution by both parties.

## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Scope of Services

Provider agrees to furnish to Client the managed IT services ("Services") as specified in the accompanying Service Order(s). Such Services may include, but are not limited to, network management, system administration, security monitoring, backup services, and technical support.

### 1.2 Service Level Commitments

Provider commits to delivering Services according to the service levels specified in Schedule A of this Agreement. These commitments include response times, system availability, and problem resolution timeframes.

### 1.3 Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to provide BlueBotPC with necessary access, information, and cooperation required to deliver the Services effectively. This includes maintaining necessary licenses, providing accurate contact information, and ensuring authorized personnel are available when required.

## 2. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 2.1 Initial Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of twelve (12) months unless otherwise specified in the Service Order.

### 2.2 Renewal

Following the initial term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve-month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the current term.

### 2.3 Contract Break Clause

Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days written notice to the other party. In the event of early termination by Client, Client agrees to pay an early termination fee equal to 50% of the remaining contract value or three months of service fees, whichever is greater.

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Service Fees

Client agrees to pay the fees specified in the Service Order(s). Unless otherwise stated, all fees are in USD and exclusive of applicable taxes.

### 3.2 Payment Processing

1. Invoices will be issued monthly in advance for recurring services.
1. Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date.
1. Provider accepts payment via ACH transfer, credit card, or check.
1. Automatic payment methods are required for all service plans.

### 3.3 Late Payments

1. Payments received after the due date will incur a late fee of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.
1. Provider reserves the right to suspend Services if payment is more than forty-five (45) days past due.
1. Client shall be responsible for all costs of collection, including reasonable attorney's fees.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 4.1 Definition

"Confidential Information" includes all non-public information disclosed by either party to the other party, including but not limited to technical data, trade secrets, and business information.

### 4.2 Protection

Both parties agree to protect Confidential Information using the same degree of care used to protect their own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 5.1 Ownership

Each party retains all rights, title, and interest in and to its own intellectual property. No license or other rights are granted except as expressly provided in this Agreement.

### 5.2 Work Product

Any custom configurations, scripts, or documentation created by Provider in the course of providing Services shall remain the property of Provider.

## 6. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### 6.1 Limitation of Liability

Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the amount paid by Client for Services in the twelve months preceding the incident giving rise to the claim.

### 6.2 Indemnification

Each party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other party from any third-party claims arising from the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement.

## 7. INSURANCE

Provider shall maintain appropriate insurance coverage, including professional liability and cyber liability insurance, throughout the term of this Agreement.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [North Dakota], without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

## 9. MODIFICATIONS

This Agreement may only be modified by written amendment signed by authorized representatives of both parties.
